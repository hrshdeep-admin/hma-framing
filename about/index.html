Our Story

Who We Are

H.M.A. Framing & Renovations Ltd. was founded in 2016 with a simple mission: to deliver exceptional construction services that transform spaces and exceed expectations. What started as a small framing operation has grown into a comprehensive construction company serving communities across Alberta.

Based in Okotoks, we've built our reputation on a foundation of quality craftsmanship, honest communication, and unwavering commitment to our clients. Every project we undertake is treated with the same level of dedication, whether it's a custom home frame or a complete commercial renovation.
